What is SparkleShip?

SparkleShip is a scholarship application platform that helps students apply to scholarships using the scholarship link, the scholarships' information is collected using a crawler, the data is then processed using OpenAI Chat-GPT API to produce a JSON file consisting of all the attributes of the processed scholarships, the scholarship information is then stored in the Database.

SparkleShip recommends scholarships to students based on the student's profile, and then shows matching scholarships that are suitable for the student.

SparkleShip compares the information of the student against the information of the browsed scholarship, and then using a weighting system (where the field of study is more important than hours of work experience etc) shows a matching percentage, this percentage helps the student gauge whether or not they meet the requirements of the scholarship and hence whether or not they should apply and determine the possibility of them getting accepted at set scholarship.

SparkleShip provides group chats, where students from different backgrounds can come and ask or provide answers to fellow students helping them achieve their goals and dreams of continuing academic studies internationally outside their home land.

SparkleShip was a Junior project built in a single semester at AIU (Arab International University).
